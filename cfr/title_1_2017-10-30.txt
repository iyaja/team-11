This part establishes rules that supplement the Council on Environmental Quality's (CEQ) National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) regulations that the National Capital Planning Commission (NCPC or Commission) and its applicants shall follow to ensure: (a) Compliance with NEPA, as amended (42 U.S.C. 4321et seq.) and CEQ regulations for implementing the procedural provisions of NEPA (40 CFR parts 1501 through 1508). (b) Compliance with other laws, regulations, and Executive Orders identified by NCPC as applicable to a particular application.
